The Creative server is accessible to all members and features WorldEdit, Axiom, and Carpet Mod. It is primarily used for designing and testing redstone contraptions, optimizing farm rates, and creating decorative elements for farms. Creative is an almost exact copy of Main, with which we use to map and plan out new farms and builds.
