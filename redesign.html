Who we are

Built on
Redstone.

Redstone Technical Farms Mob is a technical Minecraft community dedicated to farming every item in the game at the fastest possible rates. Through innovation, optimization, and collaboration, our members push the limits of Minecraft's mechanics to develop the most efficient farms and contraptions ever built.

Everything on the main server is hand-built in survival. No shortcuts. Every block placed with intention.
